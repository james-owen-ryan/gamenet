GameNet is a tool for game discovery created by UC Santa Cruz PhD students James Ryan and Eric Kaltman, as part of the larger Game Metadata and Citation Project (GAMECIP). It belongs to a suite of related tools that can all be found here.

For technical details about how GameNet works and other information, see our FAQ, or feel free to contact us.

This tool was made possible in part by Institute of Museum and Library Services grant LG-06-13-0205-13.
